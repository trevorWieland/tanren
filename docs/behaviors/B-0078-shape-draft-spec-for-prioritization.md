## Intent

A `solo-builder` or `team-builder` can shape a draft spec until it has enough product detail to be prioritized.

## Preconditions

- The spec exists in draft state.
- The user has permission to edit the spec.

## Observable outcomes

- The spec has a clear problem statement.
- The spec has acceptance criteria or an explicit reason they remain open.
- The spec can move toward backlog or ready state when shaping is complete.

## Out of scope

- Starting implementation.
- Choosing the exact implementation plan.
