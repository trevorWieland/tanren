## Intent

A `solo-builder` or `team-builder` can turn an unclear product idea into an initial project brief so planning begins from explicit intent.

## Preconditions

- An active project or prospective project context is selected.
- The user has permission to edit product planning context.

## Observable outcomes

- Tanren records the problem, audience, desired outcome, and open questions.
- Unclear parts of the idea remain visible instead of being guessed as fact.
- The brief can feed mission, roadmap, and spec creation work.

## Out of scope

- Deciding product direction without user confirmation.
- Generating implementation specs directly from an unreviewed idea.
