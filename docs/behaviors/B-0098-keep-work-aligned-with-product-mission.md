## Intent

A user can compare roadmap, specs, and candidate work against the product mission so Tanren helps preserve product coherence over time.

## Preconditions

- The project has product mission context.
- The user has visibility of the work being reviewed.

## Observable outcomes

- The user can see when work links to product mission goals.
- Work with unclear mission alignment is visible for review.
- Users can update mission links as product direction changes.

## Out of scope

- Automatically rejecting work solely because alignment is uncertain.
- Replacing human product decisions.
